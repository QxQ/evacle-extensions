undo close

allows you to undo closing a tab.

when you close a tab, a undo button appears in the address bar and stays there for 10 seconds. if you accidentally close one to many tabs, just click it to undo closing it. that simple.

created by Evacle (theQxQ@gmail.com). please comment if you found any glitches or if you have some ideas you want to see in this extension.
